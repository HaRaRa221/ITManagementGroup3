Here comes the project charter

The project charter is a document that formally authorizes a project. The project charter provides a preliminary delineation of roles and responsibilities, outlines the project objectives, identifies the main stakeholders, and defines the authority of the project manager. It serves as a reference of authority for the future of the project.

The project charter is usually a short document that refers to more detailed documents such as a new project proposal or a business case.
